The Spice Pups are a group Chi-Weenies (Chihuahua mixed with Weener dogs (Dachschund). Their names are Cinnamon, Basil and Rosemary. They love each other and their dads very much.

Cinnamon is the eldest Spice Pup. She is a very reserved, and regal puppy. However, when times call for it, she can show her feisty side with a quick nip to her siblings if they are getting out of control. Affectionately, called "Baby girl," she still retains the princess side of her personality.

Basil is the middle child. Once upon a time, she was the youngest and the most crazy. Once called "The Little One," she is no longer the youngest with the arrival of her younger sister Rosemary. Now the awkward middle child, Basil is similar to the aliens in MIB.

Rosemary is the latest addition to our pack. She is the youngest, and as expected, the most crazy and active. She has only been part of the pack for a small amount the time but already runs the show. An avid whiner, she will let you know when she is angry or when she is sad.
