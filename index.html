Ruff Ruff! Hello friends!

Welcome to our webpage! We are the Spice Pups! Feel free to click around our home and meet us! We hope you enjoy your stay and make sure to tell our dad what he can do to make your stay more enjoyable!

The Spice Pups are our pack of Chi-Weenies (Chihuahua mixed with Dachschund). Each of their stories are listed below! Come meet the Pups!

Cinnamon is the eldest Spice Pup. She is usually reserved, yet affectionate. A true Chi-Weenie, she loves being cuddled and, of course, food!

When times call for it, "Baby Girl" can show her feisty side with a quick nip to her siblings if they are getting out of control.

Basil is our middle child. We're not quite sure whether she is a true Chiweenie, or a Chi-Whippet. Once the youngest of the pack, she was not too excited when Rosemary came along.

Basil's love for the spotlight has earned her the nickname "The Thunder Stealer" from her nana.

Rosemary is the latest addition to our pack. She is the youngest, and as expected, the most crazy! Rosie was found in the rain at a nearby park. She came crawling out of a bush and straight into our hearts.

She has only been part of the pack for a small amount the time but already runs the show. An avid whiner, she will let you know when something is on her mind.
